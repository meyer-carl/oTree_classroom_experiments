Instructions

This is a two-sided auction with one buyer and one seller. The buyer has a private value for one unit. The seller has a private cost.

Both players submit a price at the same time. If the buyer's bid is at least the seller's ask, a trade occurs. The trade price is the midpoint of the two offers.

If you trade:
Buyer payoff = value - price
Seller payoff = price - cost
If no trade occurs, payoff is 0.
