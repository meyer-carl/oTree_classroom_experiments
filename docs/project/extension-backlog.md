# Extension Backlog

## Recommended Near-Term Ideas

- Endowment effect / willingness to accept vs willingness to pay.
- Gift exchange or reciprocal labor-market task.
- Common-pool resource extraction.
- Asset market bubble or continuous double auction.
- Ambiguity aversion or loss-aversion module on top of the existing risk/time preference app.

## Later Ideas

- Discrimination.
- Voting and public choice.
- Matching and market design.
- Self-control and commitment.
- Overconfidence and calibration.

## Selection Rule

Choose the next app based on classroom value, not novelty. If it is hard to explain in a live class, it should move down the queue.
