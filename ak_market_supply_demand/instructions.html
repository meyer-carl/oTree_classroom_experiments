Seller costs (low to high): {{ seller_schedule_csv }}.

Buyers submit a bid (the most you will pay). Sellers submit an ask (the least you will accept). Bids and asks are collected at the same time. Orders are sorted to find trades.

Trades happen when a bid is at least as high as a matching ask. All trades use a single clearing price based on the marginal bid and ask.

If you trade, your payoff is:
Buyer payoff = value - price
Seller payoff = price - cost
If you do not trade, your payoff is 0.
