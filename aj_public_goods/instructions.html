Instructions

You will be randomly and anonymously grouped into a group of {{ actual_group_size }}. Each player receives an endowment of {{ C.ENDOWMENT }} tokens. Each player chooses how many tokens to contribute to a public pot.

{{ if use_flexible_groups }}

This flexible classroom preset allows mixed group sizes. The target size is {{ target_group_size }}, but your actual group size this round is {{ actual_group_size }}.

{{ endif }}

After everyone in the group has made their choice: 1) all contributions in the public pot are summed up, and 2) the public pot is multiplied by {{ effective_multiplier }}, and 3) the resulting amount is split equally among the {{ actual_group_size }} group members.

Payoff formula (for each player):

Your payoff = (endowment − your contribution) + (multiplied_pot / {{ actual_group_size }})

where multiplied_pot = (sum of all contributions) * {{ effective_multiplier }}.

Contributions are anonymous. Your payoff depends on the contributions of the other group members, but you will not know who contributed what.

You will play this game for {{ C.NUM_ROUNDS }} rounds.
